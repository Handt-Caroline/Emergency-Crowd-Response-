OUR MISSION

Connecting people in crisis with care that reaches them

In an emergency, the first few minutes are everything. Yet most bystanders don't know how to quickly contact the right hospital, communicate the severity of the situation, or navigate to emergency care under stress.

ECRS was designed to solve exactly that. A single tap triggers an intelligent dispatch engine that locates the nearest available hospital, notifies their dashboard in real time, and guides the bystander — all before traditional emergency calls could even connect.

We built ECRS for Cameroon and communities like it, where the gap between an incident and medical response can be wide. We are closing that gap, one alert at a time.

GPS-Powered Dispatch
Automatically locates the nearest available hospital and routes the alert there instantly.
Real-Time Alerts
Socket.IO delivers incoming emergencies to hospital dashboards with zero delay.
Works on Any Device
Built as a Progressive Web App — no app store required, installable, works offline.
Photo Evidence
Bystanders can attach scene photos to give hospitals a fuller picture before arrival.

WHAT DRIVES US

Our Values

The principles that guide every decision we make at ECRS.

Speed Above All

Every feature is designed with response time in mind. A beautiful interface is worthless if it's slow. We obsess over milliseconds because lives depend on them.

Human-First Design

Our users are in panic when they open ECRS. Every button, every screen, and every word is designed for high-stress, low-cognition scenarios. Simplicity is our safety feature.

Trust & Reliability

ECRS must work when it matters most — in poor network conditions, on low-end devices, and in real emergencies. We build for resilience, not demos.
